Who is Tanique Adams?


I’m Tanique Adams, a Data Scientist specialized in Neuroscience. I graduated from Rider University with a B.S. in Behavioral Neuroscience and for nearly all my time there, I worked in the lab of a Molecular Biologist/Geneticist as a research assistant.


Following this, I started at Trinity School of Medicine. While I ultimately realized this was not my desired path, in my two years there I became well versed in medical terminology, which makes me well suited for research in a medical capacity as well.


In May 2019, I graduated from Rutgers' Data Science Bootcamp and recieved a Certificate of Data Science and Visualization. As a result, I’m highly efficient in Excel, Python, MySQL, MongoDB, Tableau and a wide range of additional programs. I have an extensive portfolio with my work from my time in the program, which I believe displays why I’d be an asset to any organization dealing with data manipulation, analysis and visualization. However, Neuroscience is where my passion lies, and so I intend to enter a related Neuro Data field and improve people’s lives through science and medicine. This is my greatest desire.
